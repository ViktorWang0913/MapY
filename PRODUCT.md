# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Independent game designers and level designers planning dense 2D exploration maps on desktop.

## Product Purpose

MapY turns world structure, room geometry, identifiers, connections, and notes into an editable map document. Success means designers can move quickly between high-level world planning and precise grid editing without losing ownership of the document.

## Brand Personality

Focused, technical, dependable.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style dashboards, decorative AI interfaces, oversized cards, and workflows that hide document changes behind automation.

## Design Principles

- Keep the map canvas and document model authoritative.
- Make automation inspectable and reversible.
- Prefer compact, familiar editor controls.
- Preserve manual workflows when adding assisted workflows.
- Show destructive consequences before applying them.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard access, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, readable contrast, and labels that do not rely on color alone.
